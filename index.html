International Women's Day-8 March

Women's Day is observed every year to honour the contributions of women across diverse fields. The day serves to raise awareness about gender equality, promote empowerment, and advocate for equal pay. It also recognizes women's social, economic, cultural, and political accomplishments while championing their rights worldwide.

Women's Day 2025: Theme

In 2025, the theme for International Women's Day is For ALL Women and Girls: Rights. Equality. Empowerment. This year's theme calls for action to unlock equal rights, power, and opportunities for women and a feminist future where everyone is treated equally.
